What programs do I use?

There are lots of available programs for you to choose from. Some of them are;

Adobe Photoshop is one of the best options you'll have. Supports both MacOS and Windows, but price is the main factor to be considered. I currently use this as my main software.

GIMP is of the best free options out there, and it's open source too. Supports Windows, OSX and Linux

Gravit Designer - I used this to create infographics on aircrafts. The free subscription is enough for your needs
